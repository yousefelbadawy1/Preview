About Amara

Just near Suez, our small family farm has been growing pure, organic jojoba for over five years.
With minimal intervention and full attention, we focus on quality over scale.
Every bottle reflects care, honesty, and a deep respect for nature.
Our oil is made for those who value healthy, nourished hair — naturally.

Jojoba oil is nature's closest match to the oils our skin naturally produces.
Lightweight and deeply nourishing, it absorbs effortlessly — leaving no residue,
only softness. Used regularly, it helps strengthen hair, soothe the scalp,
and restore natural shine. Gentle enough for daily care, yet powerful in results,
it's a pure, timeless remedy for healthier hair and skin.
